↪ Space Wizards is a game containing 3 groups of atleast 3-5 people travelling through outerspace conquering planets. Each planet has a correspondng Math problem. Team members have to work together and solve Math problems to conquer planets.
Each team will have 5 minutes to conquer as many planets/collect as much water that they can. Every planet they conquer equals to 50 litres of water.
The team with the most amount of water wins!
